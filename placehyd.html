CHARMINAR

Charminar is one of the oldest monuments in India.Charminar was built in 1591 and is a global symbol of Hyderabad is derived from Urdu, meaning'Four Pillars'

RAMOJI FILM CITY

Ramoji Film City is an integrated film studio facility located in Hyderabad,it is the largest film studio complex in the world and as such has been certified by the Guinness World Records.

GOLCONDA FORT

Golconda Fort is located in the western part of Hyderabad city and is about 9 km from the Hussain Sagar Lake. It was originally known as Mankal, and built on a hilltop in the year 1143.
